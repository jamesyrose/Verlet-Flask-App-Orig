Common Questions

Is it normal for queries to take a long time?

Yes, fetching large amounts of data takes a while.

I am working on a start up with very little money. Are your prices Negotiable?

Yes. Verlet is willing to work with any start up, student orgs, master/PhD students, and many others

Is your data adjusted for splits?

No, by default we do not adjust the data. We provide split dates and ratio's, along with dividends at request.

Do you have real time quotes?

No, we only offer historical data. It is updated at the end of every bussiness day.

If we want to update purchased data, do we retain the origional discount?

Currently not. Verlet is working on making daily updates possible through a subscription plan.

I accidentally lost my data, can I get it back?

Yes, it will be avaliable immediately within 24hrs of purchase. Otherwise, you many request it again at http://www.verlet.io/redownload.
